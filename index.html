Mission & Pillars

Open Care makes trusted medical information and access pathways available to every community in Bangladesh.

Core Pillars

  • Provider directory and verified profiles
  • Telemedicine and video consultation (planned)
  • Prescriptions, reports, and health vitals
  • Emergency, blood, and fundraising workflows
  • Dynamic ads and public health messaging

People & Roles

  • Patient
  • Doctor
  • Hospital Admin
  • Diagnostic Center Admin
  • Social Org Admin
  • System Admin

Data Governance

  • Privacy-first data handling
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Config-driven feature flags
  • Audit trail coverage
