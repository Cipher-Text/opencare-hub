One-Liner

Open Care Bangladesh is a privacy-first digital health ecosystem connecting patients, verified providers, and community support across Bangladesh.

Short Bio

Open Care Bangladesh organizes verified healthcare providers, hospitals, diagnostics, and community support into one trusted access point. We focus on patient-controlled sharing, RBAC, and audit trails while improving discovery and referral pathways.

Long Bio

Open Care Bangladesh is building a trusted digital health ecosystem that helps patients navigate providers, facilities, and community services with clarity. Our platform emphasizes verified profiles, privacy-first data handling, role-based access control, and audit trails. We collaborate with hospitals, diagnostic centers, doctors, and social organizations to streamline care access and public health messaging across Bangladesh.
