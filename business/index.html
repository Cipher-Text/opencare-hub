Business Plan

A diversified revenue model and phased go-to-market strategy built for a privacy-first healthcare platform in Bangladesh.

Pitch Hub Platform Modules

Executive Summary

Open Care connects patients, doctors, nurses, hospitals, diagnostics, and social organizations through verified access pathways, RBAC, and audit trails.

Focus: sustainable growth without compromising trust or affordability.

Revenue Model

Diversified streams to balance growth and long-term sustainability.

Transactional Fees

Commissions on appointments, home nursing, diagnostics, telehealth (planned), and medical fundraising.

Subscriptions

Tiered premium plans for providers and institutions with enhanced visibility and tools.

Advertising

Dynamic, location-based ads and featured listings with clear privacy controls.

Value-Added Services

Integrations, analytics insights (aggregated), and professional education programs.

Partnerships

Logistics, diagnostics, and ecosystem collaborations tied to service delivery.

Ethical Governance

Privacy-first policies with RBAC and audit trails supporting trust-based monetization.

Pricing Strategy

  • Freemium access for patients
  • Tiered provider plans (basic → premium)
  • Transparent transactional fees by service
  • Featured listings and sponsored content

Go-to-Market (Phased)

  • Phase 1: Dhaka + Chattogram onboarding
  • Phase 2: Nationwide expansion + feature rollout
  • Phase 3: South Asia pilots with localization
  • Trust-driven verification and local support

Financial Outlook (Illustrative)

  • 5-year projections based on phased growth
  • Profitability expected by Year 3 (execution dependent)
  • Costs scale across product, verification, and GTM

Figures are directional and subject to market conditions.

Funding Use

  • Product & technology expansion (40%)
  • Marketing & growth (30%)
  • Team expansion (20%)
  • Operations & compliance (10%)
