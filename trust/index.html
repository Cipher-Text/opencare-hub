Safety & Trust Commitments

Open Care Bangladesh is built for responsible healthcare access. Our policies focus on verified providers, moderated content, and patient-controlled data sharing.

Contact Trust Team Report an Issue

Medical Disclaimer

Information on Open Care is for general awareness and does not provide a diagnosis. If you are facing a medical emergency, call local emergency services or go to the nearest hospital in Bangladesh immediately.

We do not replace in-person clinical care.

Doctor Verification Policy

Verified doctors must submit professional credentials and evidence of practice in Bangladesh.

  • BMDC registration details and valid ID
  • Clinic/hospital affiliation or chamber documents
  • Manual review by the Open Care verification team
  • Periodic rechecks and random audits

Review & Content Moderation

Reviews, blogs, and public posts are monitored for accuracy and abuse.

  • Automated flags for harmful or misleading content
  • Human review for reported posts and reviews
  • No personal attacks, harassment, or misinformation
  • Repeat abuse leads to removal or suspension

Privacy Principles

  • Patient-controlled sharing of health data
  • Minimal data collection for core services
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Audit trails for sensitive actions

Reporting & Escalation

We encourage the community to report fake profiles, misinformation, or harmful behavior.

  • Report via in-app flow or email
  • Urgent cases prioritized for same-day review
  • Escalation to verification team or leadership
  • Actions logged with audit trails
