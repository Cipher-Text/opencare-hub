Product Vision & MVP

A modular, role-based platform that builds trusted medical access for Bangladesh while preparing for telemedicine, analytics, and community support.

Product Vision

  • Single digital gateway for care access and verified providers
  • Community support: emergency, blood, and fundraising
  • Future telemedicine workflows with secure data handling
  • Multi-role dashboards and config-driven feature flags

Roadmap (Phase 1–4)

Phase 1

Directory, verified profiles, and advertisement MVP.

Phase 2

Telemedicine POC, appointment workflows, initial analytics.

Phase 3

Prescriptions, reports, health vitals tracking, and patient dashboard.

Phase 4

Emergency/blood network, fundraising tools, and ecosystem automation.

Phase 4+

Regional expansion, advanced analytics, and partner integrations.

Continuous

Security, RBAC updates, and privacy-first policy refinement.
